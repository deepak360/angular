FIFA World Cup 2018 Schedule, Fixtures

FIFA World Cup 2018 is scheduled to take place in Russia from 14 June to 15 July 2018. The final tournament will involve 32 national teams with 8 groups. Each group will involve 4 national teams. A total of 64 matches will be played in 12 venues located in 11 cities. The final will take place on 15 July in Moscow at the Luzhniki Stadium.
